About Me

I am a PhD Candidate in Finance at Columbia Business School. My main research interest is corporate finance theory.

In particular, I am interested in how the institutional design of bankruptcy and restructuring affect each other, how the asset and cash flow pledegability affects a firm's ability to obtain liquidity insurance.

My Research

Restructuring and Bankruptcy

with Ed R. Morrison, Giorgia Piacentino and Jason R. Donaldson

We develop a model of a firm in financial distress. Distress can be mitigated by filing for bankruptcy (which is costly) or preempted by restructuring (which is impeded by a collective action problem).
